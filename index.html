Delay Fees and No Refund Policies

Delay Fees Policy

In case of delays in processing transactions:

  • A delay fee will be charged for each day of delay.
  • The delay fee will not be deducted from the transaction amount.
  • This fee is subject to change without prior notice unless approved.

No Refund Policy

Our company follows a strict no refund policy under the following circumstances:

  • Refunds will not be provided for completed transactions.
  • No refunds will be issued for services rendered.
  • Exceptions may be considered on a case-by-case basis.
